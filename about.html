About Us

Little Stepping Stones is a privately owned daycare centre/creche located in Bhambanana, right next to uMfolozi College Bhambanana Campus. . It was created to bridge a gap as there was a shortage of independent creches in the area to produce what we can call quality education and child care.

Mission and Vision

Promote effective, affordable models of early childhood education that will support and strengthen our communities, which offers early childhood development programme. Feed to our nearest private primary schools with quality developed learners by employing suitable, qualified practitioners and forming good relationships with parents.
